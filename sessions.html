Weight Management Consultation

  If you are seeking to reduce your risk of developing health conditions, it is a must to manage your weight within a healthy range. Embarking on this journey demands the right type of support to increase your chances for success, and our weight management consultation is a good starting point to have access to the expert you need who can get you on path to your weight loss journey.
For this session, a certified nutritionist or registered dietician will take the time to understand your weight loss struggles and will take a look at your medical condition and diet history. These are done in order to get good sense of where you are at in your weight loss journey and where you want to go. The expert who will work with you will also help you in identifying potential barriers to your weight loss.

Based on the information you provide, the expert will discuss weight loss strategies that are suited to your needs. These may include, but are not limited to, goal-setting, diet plans, healthy lifestyle education for long-term success, and medical supervision recommendation (if needed).

Nutrition Counselling  

Staying fit and healthy is not just about having a customised workout program. It is also about having a well-balanced diet that is necessary for performing daily activities, growth and tissue repair, immune system reinforcement, chronic disease prevention, etc. A good nutrition counselling is exactly what you need if you want to incorporate healthy changes into your diet and lifestyle.
A quick session with a certified nutritionist or registered dietician can be a good start to help you adopt a healthier lifestyle. The expert you will be working with will understand your goals and objectives and will ask things related to your medical history, lifestyle, dietary preferences and more. By discussing these points, the expert can identify trouble spots that need your attention and offer some initial pointers to incorporate simple changes that make sense for your lifestyle.  

Nutrition counselling can be a cost-effective solution that will benefit you for the long-term. By fine-tuning your food choices and making changes to your diet, you can save thousands of pounds in medications if you are managing a medical condition or avoid hospitalisation altogether.

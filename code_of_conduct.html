Code Of Conduct



Our code of conduct defines how all of us here at Experiencely operate on a daily basis. It gives emphasis on the values we maintain as well as the standards we follow in the delivery of our service and the way we treat those who use our platform. Put it simply, it is a reflection of who we are as a company.



Purpose



Our main goal is to provide both individuals and fitness experts a more convenient, flexible, safe and economical way of engaging with each other. As such, we make sure that our platform is open to everyone who would like to get fit and healthy, regardless of their gender, ability, sexual orientation, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, and religion or lack thereof.


The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to let those using our platform to understand how we operate, what are our expectations when they use our service, and what are the consequences when they engage in unacceptable behaviour.


Expected Behaviour



All users of our platform are expected and requested to conduct themselves in such a manner that ensures respect towards other users and does not interfere with the orderly operation of the platform.

  • Fitness experts are expected to be competent, honest, and conducts themselves in ways that accord respect to themselves and others.
  • Fitness experts are expected to take full responsibility for their actions and to strive to serve clients fairly.
  • Fitness experts are expected to be fully aware of their responsibilities, capably and appropriately communicate their services and contribute constructively to the progress of the clients they serve.
  • Both fitness experts and users/clients must refrain from engaging in demeaning, discriminatory and/or harassing behaviours and speech.
  • Everyone is expected to cultivate and maintain a positive, safe and supportive environment.


Unacceptable Behaviour



Listed below are behaviours that we deem unacceptable when using our platform.


  • Online bullying
  • Inappropriate comments (sexist, homophobic, transphobic, racist, ableist, discriminatory)
  • Uploading or displaying inappropriate materials (adult content, illegal, anti-social)
  • Posting or threatening to post the personally identifiable information of other people.
  • Deliberate intimidation, stalking or following.
  • Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviour.


    • Consequences



    Consequences are in line for those who fail to adhere to this Code of Conduct. Minor infractions are dealt with temporary suspension of account. Repeat offences will result in permanent removal of account.


    Taking Action


    If you have witnessed unacceptable behaviour or are a subject of it, it is your responsibility to TAKE ACTION. Keep in mind that not taking action against unacceptable behaviour can have serious consequences to those affected, the platform, and the community. As such, immediately notify us about the problem as soon as possible by sending us an email or by calling us.

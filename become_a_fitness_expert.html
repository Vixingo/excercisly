Minimum requirements to become a
fitness expert at FitnessExpert

Availability

We give you the flexibility to decide how much time you want to invest in training or coaching clients. Depending on the services you provide, you can spend an hour a week or more for each client.

Experience

We want to build a community of the best fitness experts who extensive experience in the industry and are willing to use their competencies to help clients in our platform achieve amazing results.

Communications

Clients in our platform value communication. As a fitness expert, we want you to be ready to speak over the phone or engage in video calls to answer questions and provide support to people in their health journey.

Openness and Empathy

As a fitness expert, you will be dealing with people from all sorts of backgrounds and situations. This makes it important to provide clients with assistance and solutions without passing judgment on who they are and what situation they are in.

Setting yourself up and your services for success

Dedicated Profile

As one of our fitness experts, you will have your own dedicated profile that will be visible to the public. Complete your profile with details about your areas of specialisations, services and the rate you would like to charge. You can also set up one-off session that client's can avail, such as a 12-week nutrition and diet plan.

Select Clients

AAll clients are required to fill out a form with information about their fitness needs, goals, preferred service, lifestyle, fitness level and more when reaching out to a fitness expert. You can use the information they provide as a reference to select clients that you can work with based on your preference and availability.

3 Days Free Trial

You must be able to offer a 3-day free trial to clients. This will give them an idea how the , and at the same time make them feel confident in the service you offer. The free trial can include a quick phone consultation to learn more about them and what they want to achieve, and providing them a sample diet plan or workout plan to set their fitness journey in motion.

You can find a management tool within your profile to communicate with your clients and check up on their progress. In case you have agreed on another medium of communication with your clients, that will be fine, too.

Platform and System

  As someone interested to get fit and healthy, you can use our platform to search for personal trainers, fitness coaches, nutritionists and dieticians who can guide and support you in achieving your fitness goals. Their profiles are visible to the public. You can go over them to know more about each fitness expert’s areas of specialization, services and rates, among others.

  Once you have chosen your preferred fitness expert, you need to book their services through their profile. Take note that a fitness expert can accept or reject your booking based on their availability. If a fitness expert accepts your booking, you will be asked to make an authorised payment. After which, you will be able to get in touch with your chosen fitness expert and start with your personal training or fitness coaching.

It doesn’t really matter if you are a total beginner or have advanced experience doing fitness training. You can use our platform whatever fitness level you are in. Majority of the fitness experts in our platform can cater to different clients and have years of experience designing programs around specific needs and goals. This means it will not be difficult to find one that suits your requirements.

All services offered by out fitness experts are delivered online. This should allow you to do your workouts or receive expert coaching in the privacy of your own home. You can also book a fitness expert around your fitness needs, goals and level as well as your schedule. There are no contracts or membership fees to worry about.  

Payment

  All our fitness experts have control over their service rate. You can see this on their dedicated profile, along with the range of services they provide. The rate set by a fitness expert is the amount you need to pay for ongoing personal training or fitness coaching. Take note that the amount you will be paying for is not just for the service offered by the expert itself, but also their availability, experience and expertise. If you are paying for a higher rate, then you should expect to get more services from your chosen expert.

This will depend on the fitness expert you have chosen, as they might be in another country where other payment methods apply. In most cases, however, other users in the platform are prompted to pay by credit card or PayPal.

We handle refunds on a case-to-case basis given that not all our fitness experts provide the same type of service. We may provide a refund if you encounter any of the following:  

  • Your fitness expert has not checked in on you in about a week without prior notification.
  • Your fitness expert demonstrated misconduct or breach of trust.
  • Your fitness expert failed to provide you with agreed services, resulting to a breach in the service agreement.
  • Your fitness expert decided to give a refund for whatever reason.

We will not provide a refund if you:
  • Believe that the progress you are making is not good enough.
  • Are unable to reach your fitness goals in time.
  • Think the personal training or fitness coaching service is not delivering the results you are after.

We understand that not all personal training or fitness coaching programs are created equal. Some may go smoothly, while others may not. We always strive to deliver the best possible service, so we try to work out a solution that will satisfy both fitness experts and clients who use our platform.

Sessions  

  A session is a one-off engagement with a fitness professional that is designed to deliver prompt solution to a fitness challenge. A session can run anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour and scope varies from workout program planning to nutrition counselling.  

Depending on your fitness concern, the fitness expert you will work with will answer any question you might have and may offer advice, feedback or an actionable plan. Because it has a fixed duration, the coverage of your discussion may be limited so you may need to book another session with your chosen fitness expert or you may proceed with a weekly or monthly service. To do the latter, go to the dedicated profile of your preferred fitness expert and book them on a long-term basis.

Choose from our list of sessions and from the roster of fitness experts offering your preferred session. Book that fitness expert and wait from an email from the expert for a schedule of your session.
